PantherChat is an online financial aid chat experience which allows students and employees from college financial aid offices to communicate with one another via text Q&A. The creators and planners of this excellent program are Brett Duncan and Kamari Cook. Brett had the idea of creating a more efficient way of reciving financial aid information without the hassel of long waits on the phone while on hold. Kamari liked the idea and decided to work with Brett. Brett primarily worked on the coding while Kamari primarily worked on the design, planning and some coding.
